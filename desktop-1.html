ABOUT US

Waste management refers to the collection, transport, processing, and disposal of waste materials. This can includes practices such as recycling, composting, and safe disposal of hazardous waste.

The need for waste management arises from the increasing amount of waste produced by human activities, such as consumption, industrial processes, and population growth. This waste, if not properly managed, can have negative impacts on the environment and public health.

Our moto is firmly based on the following few words: "Reduce, Reuse, Recycle", "Think globally, act locally", "Clean environment, healthy living", This highlights the importance of proper waste management in maintaining a healthy environment and promoting overall well-being.
